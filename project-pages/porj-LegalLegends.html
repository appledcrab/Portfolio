My Project Experiences

Going into 2024, I felt that I hadn't done enough or tried enough things. While that was vauge, I stilled in myself that I needed to do more things that Spring semester. That meant participating in clubs more, going to events, and doing more projects.

As such, this was the beggining. Global Game Jam was my first hackathon type of event of working under a hard deadline. It was stressful, showing up to the event with no people in mind to work with, but a small group got together after the initial ceremony and we were off.

Our game was a 2d pixel art game where you play as a lawyer in a courtroom. The game was a mix of Ace Attorney with a funny LoL reference, where you had to find contradictions in the witness's statements. The game was a bit buggy, but we were able to get a working prototype by the end of the event.

Lessons Learned

Through this project, I continued to see how much people can do in a short amount of time. I felt that I could really contribute to something and that I could work with a team more closely.

I was also surprised with how much art I could make in 36 hours.

Future Plans

After the event was over, we continued until we had a fully voiced game which we then pushed to Itch.io!

We enjoyed working on it and while we had tried to get a fully voiced game during the alloted time, we did not reach that goal. We did get most of the characters voiced and got to put in all of 3 characters voices, but removed it before submitting as we couldn't just have a few voiced.
