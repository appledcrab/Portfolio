Unity 3d 2d Pixel Art Team

My Project Experiences

This project was for Aurora Club (now PantherDev) @ GSU. Out of 5 or so different groups that were showing off their different ideas, I went with this group for their interesting idea and I enjoy doing pixel art.

My role was mainly to create background assets including making repeating tiles, room assets, and general art thumbnails.

It was a great experience to work with a team and see how everyone's different skills came together to create a game. While it could have been better organized, we were all getting used to it, and if we all got together to try again we would have done better.

We had weekly meetings to checkup on how everyone was doing, but unfortunately some classes were outweighing the priority for alot of members and we ended up stalling for a bit of time.

Overall, having to work with a team and having to create assets for a game was a great experience and I would love to do it again.

Lessons Learned

Working with a group of people, having weekly meetings, meeting a deadline, and collaborating with others and having to merge all our talents.

Future Plans

I would like to try making a game again. Making a game with other people becomes more feasible than trying by myself.
